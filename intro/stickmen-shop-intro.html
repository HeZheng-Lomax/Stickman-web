What is Stickmen

The stickmen is a universally recognizable symbol. Its simplicity and versatility led to the stick figure being used for a variety of purposes: info graphics, signage, comics, animations, games, film storyboards, and many kinds of visual media all employ the stick figure.

History

The stick figure's earliest roots are in prehistoric art. Some of the most revealing and informative markers of early human life are cave paintings and petroglyphs, ancient depictions covering a variety of subjects left behind on stone walls.

In the early 1920s, Austrian sociologist Otto Neurath used Stickmen to create standardized and easily understood hieroglyphics to convey warnings, statistics, and general information.Today, Stickmen are heavily utilized in life and in the creation of art.
